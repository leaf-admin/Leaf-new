## Practical limits observed
- Hard break point (E2E): around `1000 rides / conc 250`.
- Reliability starts degrading (multi-user): around `20000/2000` with `conc >= 650`.
- Recommended steady operation (current hardware):
  - Multi-user booking load: up to `10000/1000` with `conc 500-800`.
  - E2E rides: up to `400/100` for better latency; `600/150` still acceptable with higher p95.
